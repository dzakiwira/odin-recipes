Carbonara

Description

Humble ingredients--eggs, noodles, cheese, and pork--combine to create glossy, glorious pasta carbonara. It's the no-food-in-the-house dinner of our dreams

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. kosher salt, plus more
  • 4 oz. guanciale, pancetta, or bacon
  • 2 oz. Parmesan
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. spaghetti, bucatini, or rigatoni
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat 6 qt. water and add 3 Tbsp. salt
  2. Remove 4 oz. guanciale and finely grate 2 oz. cheese
  3. Whisk 4 Egg yolks and 2 whole eggs. Add several cranks of pepper
  4. Heat 2 Tbsp. oil
  5. Remove pot from heat
  6. Cook 1 lb. pasta
  7. Drain pasta and put into dutch oven
  8. Cook pasta until al dente and no water
  9. Mix in whisked eggs
  10. Mix in guanciale and top with pepper and cheese
